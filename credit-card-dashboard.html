🎭 Storyline: Cracking the Credit Card Code

In the buzzing world of financial transactions, an unexplained drop in card usage jolted the leadership of a top bank. Transactions were up in some states, down in others, and credit limits seemed mysteriously stagnant. This wasn't just a data dip — it was a *plot twist*. Enter our Data Detective: armed with Power BI and a mountain of credit card data.

This dashboard was designed to bring complete visibility into the **Credit Card lifecycle** — from issuance to limits, customer activity, and regional insights. With crisp KPIs, slicers, and interactive visuals, the project provided:

  • 📊 Segmented overview of customers by age, gender, and credit limit
  • 📍 Geo-based performance of credit cards by state
  • 💰 Deep-dive into credit limit distributions & card types
  • 🕵️‍♂️ Strategic indicators for identifying underutilized segments

What started as a messy dataset turned into a crystal-clear visual story. With this dashboard, leadership was able to make informed decisions, personalize campaigns, and forecast user behavior with confidence.

🔧 Tools Used
