Overview

In a world where every financial decision holds the promise of transforming lives, the Bank Loan Dashboard was conceived as a beacon of clarity. This project transforms raw loan data into an immersive narrative‚Äîuncovering hidden risks, revealing emerging trends, and empowering decision-makers with actionable insights.

Our Mission

Imagine a future where every loan decision is illuminated by crystal-clear insight. Our mission is to harness the power of data to not only visualize numbers but to narrate the story behind each customer's financial journey, driving impactful and strategic decisions.

The Quest

  • Illuminate the Unknown: Capture critical KPIs‚Äîfrom surges in loan applications to subtle warnings of potential defaults.
  • Uncover Hidden Patterns: Decode the intricate relationships between loan amounts, repayment behaviors, and customer profiles.
  • Empower Decisions: Create an interactive platform that transforms raw data into actionable intelligence.

The Journey

Our journey began with a treasure trove of data stored in financial_loan.csv. Through rigorous data cleaning using SQL and drawing insights from key industry documents, we set the stage for visualization. Leveraging the dynamic capabilities of Power BI and Tableau, we crafted a dashboard that tells a vivid story of financial health.
