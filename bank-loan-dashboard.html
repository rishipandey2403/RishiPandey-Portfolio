Overview

In a world where every financial decision holds the promise of transforming lives, the Bank Loan Dashboard was conceived as a beacon of clarity. By transforming raw loan data into an immersive narrative, this project uncovers hidden risks, reveals emerging trends, and empowers decision-makers with actionable insights.

Project Details

Our Mission

Imagine a future where every loan decision is backed by crystal-clear insight. Our mission was to create an analytical masterpiece that not only visualizes data but tells the unique story of each customer's financial journey.

The Quest

  • Illuminate the Unknown: Capture critical KPIs‚Äîfrom surges in loan applications to the silent signals of potential defaults.
  • Uncover Hidden Patterns: Decode intricate relationships between loan amounts, repayment behaviors, and customer demographics.
  • Empower Decisions: Develop an interactive platform that inspires proactive, strategic decision-making.

The Journey

Our journey began with a treasure trove of data in financial_loan.csv. Through meticulous cleaning using SQL and insights drawn from domain expertise (via documents like Domain Knowledge Doc.docx and Terminologies in Data.docx), we set the stage for visualization. With the dynamic power of Power BI and Tableau, this dashboard emerged as a living narrative of financial health.

Revelations

  • Risk Unveiled: High-risk segments emerge, turning abstract figures into actionable intelligence.
  • Temporal Trends: Dynamic shifts in loan approvals highlight pivotal moments in financial strategy.
  • Correlation Chronicles: Intriguing links between metrics narrate a compelling story of borrower behavior.
