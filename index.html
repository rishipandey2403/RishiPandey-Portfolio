My Journey as a Data Storyteller

Welcome to my digital portfolio—a dynamic fusion of analytics and art. I am Rishi Pandey, a Data Analyst, Business Analyst, and Data Scientist with a passion for unearthing insights that matter. With a PGDM in Big Data Analytics from Goa Institute of Management and hands-on experience at Deloitte, I have honed my skills in transforming data into actionable narratives.

My role was to provide the analytical backbone that drives strategic decisions—helping to empower a global team to lead with data.

Every project I undertake is a story waiting to be told—a journey that starts with raw numbers and ends with transformative insights. In this portfolio, you’ll witness the evolution of data into decisions, as I blend technical expertise with creative narrative to deliver results that top Fortune 500 firms and MAANG companies are eager to harness.
