Dataset & Modelling Approach

I used season-wise IPL match and player statistics (batting, bowling and team results) and modelled it in Power BI as a clean semantic layer:

  • Match-level table ‚Äì season, teams, venue, result, total 4s, 6s, centuries, winning margin.
  • Team table ‚Äì team names, logos, abbreviations (used in points table visual).
  • Player stats table ‚Äì season-wise runs, wickets, 4s and 6s for Orange/Purple Cap logic.
  • Season dimension ‚Äì used as the main slicer for toggling 2008‚Äì2025 views.

In Power BI, I used relationships on Season and Team, and created DAX measures for aggregate KPIs (total 4s, total 6s, matches, centuries, points, etc.) so the entire page responds to a single Season dropdown.
