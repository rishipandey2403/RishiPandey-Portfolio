⚡ Project Overview: Charging Ahead with Insight

With the global pivot to sustainable mobility, understanding electric vehicle (EV) adoption trends is mission-critical. This dashboard delivers high-voltage clarity on EV ownership, model preferences, and regional adoption across the U.S.

  • 🌎 Regional EV penetration across counties and states
  • 🚗 Make & model breakdown of popular electric vehicles
  • 📈 Year-on-year adoption insights & ownership types
  • 🔍 Insights into government vs. private fleet usage

This dashboard transforms raw vehicle registration data into a sleek, interactive analytics tool that fuels strategic EV market expansion.

🧰 Tools Used

Power BI · DAX · Data Cleaning · Interactive Filters · Geo Visuals
