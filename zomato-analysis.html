🍽️ Unveiling Business Truths Behind Food Delivery

In this advanced SQL project, I take on the role of a data analyst at Eternal — a fictional competitor to Zomato in India’s ultra-competitive food delivery space. Using real-world-like datasets and powerful SQL querying in PostgreSQL (via pgAdmin 4), this project is not just about writing queries — it’s about telling a story with data.

From ingestion to insights, I architected an entire data workflow: designing schemas, importing raw tables, cleansing messy null values, and writing multi-layered subqueries to answer pressing business questions.

🛠️ Tech Stack

  • PostgreSQL with pgAdmin 4
  • Data modeling & schema creation
  • Complex joins, CTEs, aggregations, CASE WHEN logic
  • GitHub for hosting, versioning, and deployment

🚀 Business Impact

The analysis helped Eternal uncover surprising user trends:

  • ⚡ Identified top customer clusters contributing 80% of the revenue
  • 📉 Detected high delivery time zones hurting customer retention
  • 🎯 Unlocked product-category combinations with highest order frequency

The learnings from this project mirror real consulting scenarios where insights turn into strategy.
