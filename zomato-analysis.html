Zomato SQL Analysis

Category: Advanced SQL

Tech Stack: PostgreSQL (pgAdmin4), GitHub

📖 The Business Tale

Picture this: *Eternal*, a buzzing food delivery startup trying to match the reach of Zomato, is drowning in data. Orders, cancellations, customer ratings, delivery partners — all trapped in messy spreadsheets. That’s where I come in.

⚙️ The Technical Operation

I designed and implemented a structured relational database in PostgreSQL. After cleaning and prepping the data, I tackled business challenges like:

  • Top cities by order volume
  • Customer retention insights
  • Avg. delivery time by region
  • Peak hour trends
  • Missing data reconciliation using subqueries and CASE WHEN logic

📊 SQL That Drives Decisions

This wasn’t just textbook SQL. I wrote layered CTEs, window functions, JOINs across datasets, and analytical queries that brought Eternal’s performance into crystal focus.

💥 Real Impact

Through this project, I transformed raw CSVs into a decision-making engine. It’s not just SQL — it’s strategy. Recruiters, imagine what I could do with your data.
