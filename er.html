🚨 Triage in Turmoil: A Data-Driven ER Story

Picture this: a packed emergency room. Patients arriving by the minute. Limited beds. Stressed staff. Seconds count. What if data could bring calm to the chaos?

This interactive dashboard decodes the drama of emergency operations, translating life-or-death decisions into strategic insights. It's not just about numbers—it's about impact.

  • 📅 Hourly & daily patient inflow trends
  • ⏱️ Average patient wait & treatment time by triage level
  • 🏥 Resource usage, peak hours, and ER load mapping
  • 📍 Geo-patterns in emergency cases

From patient spikes to bottlenecks, this dashboard enables hospitals to proactively allocate staff, streamline operations, and ultimately save lives.

🧰 Tools Used

Power BI · Data Modelling · DAX Measures · Time Intelligence · Conditional Formatting
