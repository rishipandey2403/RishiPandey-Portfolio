My Professional Journey

I am Rishi Pandey, a 25-year-old Data Analyst, Business Analyst, and Data Scientist with a passion for transforming raw data into strategic insights. My journey began with a strong academic foundation—a PGDM in Big Data Analytics from Goa Institute of Management—where I mastered the art of analytics, and earned accolades for innovative problem solving.

At Deloitte, as a Consultant (Assistant Manager), I spearheaded transformative projects for global giants like Unilever UK, engineering data-driven solutions on Azure that boosted product performance by 40% and improved operational efficiency significantly.

Reporting Insights & Operations at American Express

In my most recent six-month stint (April–October 2025) as a Reporting Insights & Operations Intern at American Express, I was entrusted with delivering actionable insights for one of the world’s most admired financial institutions. At Amex, I navigated through complex data sets, built robust reports, and influenced strategy by translating granular data into clear, impactful narratives.

The role demanded a blend of technical acumen and business savvy—requiring expertise in Hive, SQL, Python, Tableau, and Power BI— all of which I employed to drive forward-thinking initiatives that enhanced sales performance and operational efficiency.

At Amex, the mantra was "You Lead the Way. We’ve Got Your Back." This ethos fueled my determination to excel, ensuring that every report and insight I delivered was aligned with the company's commitment to excellence.
