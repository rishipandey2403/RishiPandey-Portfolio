🚀 Dashboard Story

In a competitive auto market, split-second insights drive sales strategies. This Car Sales Dashboard arms dealers with real-time KPIs — from YTD vs PTYD growth to region-wise sales mapping.

  • 📊 Sales Overview: YTD, MTD, YOY growth & PTYD comparisons
  • 💰 Average Price Analysis: YTD/MTD averages, YOY growth
  • 🚙 Cars Sold Metrics: YTD/MTD volumes, YOY trends
  • 🌐 Geo Insights: Dealer-region map shading by sales
  • 📋 Detailed Grids: Company-wise & transactional drill-through

Users can trace weekly YTD trends, compare average prices, and pinpoint colour or body-style hotspots. Every interaction fires a DAX measure, surfacing data-backed decisions that accelerate revenue and optimize inventory.

🧰 Tools Used

Power BI · DAX · Power Query (M) · Data Modeling · KPI Dashboarding
