Tech Stack & Development Pipeline

  • Data Source: Car sales CSVs loaded into Power BI Dataflows.
  • ETL: M queries for cleansing, date-table creation, and KPI calculations (YTD, MTD, YOY, PTYD).
  • Visuals: Line, Pie, Map & Table visuals powered by DAX measures for dynamic analytics.
  • Interactivity: Slicers for time, body style, colour & region; drill-through for detail grids.
  • Deployment: Published to Power BI Service with row-level security and scheduled refresh.
